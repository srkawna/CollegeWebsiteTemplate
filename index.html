Highlights
  • Most sought after Technical Institute in Central India.
  • RCOEM has been awarded “A” Grade by NAAC for a period of 5 years from Dec,2014
  • Departments are accredited by NBA, some program earned NBA-tier I accreditation for five years.
  • MOUs with Universities of International repute of USA, UK and Australia.
  • MOUs with 31 industries, provide consultancy to various industries including WCL, MOIL, HINDALCO, Indorama, MahaGenco, M&M etc.
  • Implementing Vishvesharaya PhD Scheme of Department of Electronics and Information Technology(DeitY).
  • Institute level Admissions (Management quota) on merit and transparent basis.
  • Shri Ramdeobaba College Engineers for Environment Forum (REEF) of RCOEM is the recipient of Earthian Award-2015.
  • Highest Placements in Central India.
  • Experienced , Qualified and Dedicated faculties, PhD awarded: 78, PhD submitted: 11.
  • Well Placed Alumni. Strong tie with Alumni. The alumni association is giving scholarship to bright students in addition to Tuition fee Waiver scheme of Institute. Association also donates technical education aid.
  • RCOEM selected as Knowledge Management Partner Institution (KMPI) of Consultancy Development Centre (CDC), DSIR (Dept of Scientific and Industrial Research), Government of India under the programme “Building Capacities for Consultancy Development and Knowledge Management with Partner Institution”.
  • RCOEM was selected as the top institution in Maharashtra by RGSTC for the implementation of scheme of Technology Information, Forecasting, and Assessment Council (TIFAC), by Rajiv Gandhi Science and Technology Commission (RGSTC), Government of Maharashtra.
  • Vibrant Entrepreneurship Development Cell (OASIS) and Business Incubation Centre (First Step)
  • Institute Premises under CCTV Surveillance
